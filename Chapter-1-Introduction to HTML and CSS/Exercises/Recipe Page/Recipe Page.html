Fluffy Japanese Soufflé Pancake


This is one of my go-to pancake recipe, the Fluffy Japanese Soufflé Pancake. Unlike traditional pancakes, this pancake from Japan is known for its jiggly appearance, subtly sweetness, and light, fluffy, and cloud-like texture.

Ingredients


  • 4 Egg whites
  • 4 Egg Yolks
  • 4 Tablespoon of Milk
  • 1 Teaspoon of Vanilla Extract
  • 1⁄2 Cup of All Purpose Flour
  • 1⁄2 Teaspoon of Baking Powder
  • 4 Tablespoon of Granulated Sugar

Method

  1. Place the 4 egg yolks, 4 tablespoons of milk, and 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract into a large bowl.
  2. Add the 1⁄2 cup of all-purpose flour and 1⁄2 teaspoon of baking powder while whisking and mixing the three ingredients.
  3. In a separate bowl, place the 4 egg whites and whisk them until firm peaks form, adding four tablespoons of granulated sugar at the same time.
  4. Mix the two mixtures together.
  5. Heat up a nonstick pan over low heat and lightly brush with oil.

  6. Carefully place the mixture in the pan.
  7. Add drops of water to the pan and cover it with a lid.
  8. Cook it over low heat for 4-5 minutes on each side.
  9. Once cooked, remove it and place it on a plate.

  10. Add any toppings you would like, such as whipped cream, strawberries, honey, maple syrup, butter, or powdered sugar.

  11. Serve and enjoy immediately!
