Cast

Meet the talented cast of "Avatar - The Last Air Bender" that captivated and brought their characters from the cartoon to life.

Gordon Cormier as Aang

He was cast when he was 11 years old and already boasts several impressive Hollywood credits, including a role in Stephen King’s The Stand.

Kiawentiio as Katara

She has appeared in Netflix’s Anne With an E and Marvel’s What If…?.

Ian Ousley as Sokka

He played a role in Netflix’s 13 Reasons Why or Disney+’s Big Shot.

Dallas Liu as Zuko

He has starred in Marvel’s Shang-Chi and the Legend of the Ten Rings, Mortal Combat: Legacy, and PEN15.

Paul Sun-Hyung Lee As Uncle Iroh

He is best known for Kim’s Convenience and various Star Wars franchise appearances.

Daniel Dae Kim As Fire Lord Ozai

One of Netflix's veteran actors, he has voiced two characters in the Avatar world prior, lending his voice to Avatar - The Last Airbender Series as General Fong and Hiroshi Sato in its spinoff show The Legend of Korra.

Ken Leung as Commmander Zhao

Recently starred in Missing with Storm Reid.
